# Music Directory

Place your background music files here.

## Supported Formats

- MP3 (.mp3)
- M4A (.m4a)
- WAV (.wav)
- AAC (.aac)

## Naming Convention

Music files should be named according to their music_id:

- `default_01.mp3`
- `default_02.mp3`
- `energetic_01.mp3`
- `calm_01.mp3`
- `motivational_01.mp3`

## Requirements

- **Duration**: Any length (will be automatically trimmed or looped to 7 seconds)
- **Quality**: 128kbps or higher recommended
- **Volume**: Pre-normalized audio recommended to avoid clipping

## License Note

Ensure you have the proper license to use music in your Instagram content. Consider:

- Royalty-free music libraries
- Instagram's licensed music library (via Meta API)
- Original compositions
